How do I make sense of all this research?

Evaluative Methods

We've spent two weeks researching how users are tackling the problem you're desinging. Two weeks ago we did some ethnographic research that focused on observing what they're doing. Last week we enlisted their active participation to better understand how they approach about the problem.

This week: how do you make sense of that research? How do you synthesize it into something actionable that will inform your designs?

  • Task Analysis
  • Jobs to be Done Analysis
  • Mental Model Diagram
  • Content analysis / Content Inventory
  • Mind Mapping / Visual Organizing
  • Benchmarking / Competitive testing
  • Analogous Models
  • Heuristic Analysis *
